# Plan: 24-week curriculum — Camino a Madrid

2026-07-17 → 2026-12-31 · ≤20 min/day · no streaks, only a 60′/week floor.

**The week** (research-backed — Nation's four strands; see README):
Mon conversation (Claude voice) · Tue natural-speed listening (Hoy Hablamos) ·
Wed 4/3/2 fluency drill · Thu roleplay (Gliglish, Spain dialect) · Fri
shadowing (Español con Juan) · Sat free/catch-up · Sun rest · +5′ Anki daily.
The phase themes below feed the Mon/Wed/Sat topics and Thu scenarios.

---

## Fase 1 — Reboot

**2026-07-17 → 2026-08-31 · ~6.5 weeks (weeks 1–7)**

**Goal:** shake the rust off. Rebuild fluency on topics that used to be easy,
get `vosotros` back into automatic reach, and get comfortable inside
low-stakes Gliglish scenarios before Phase 2 raises the difficulty.

Weekly themes:

1. Reintroductions — daily life, routine, work, family catch-up
2. Home & neighborhood — apartment, errands, directions
3. Food & restaurants — ordering, preferences, dietary chat
4. Travel logistics — trains, flights, hotels, small talk with strangers
5. Health & body — pharmacy, doctor's small talk, how-are-you-feeling
6. Hobbies & weekends — plans, opinions on free time, weather chat
7. Review + `vosotros` push — mixed review week, drill `vosotros`
   conjugations daily in Anki across all the above topics

**Anki focus:** daily `vosotros` conjugation drilling threaded through every
week's vocabulary, plus the week's topical vocab.

**Gliglish:** comfortable, familiar scenarios — café orders, hotel check-in,
asking for directions. Low unpredictability, warm-up territory.

---

## Fase 2 — Expansión

**2026-09-01 → 2026-10-31 · ~8.7 weeks (weeks 8–15)**

**Goal:** move from "can get by" to "can hold an opinion." Debate, tell
stories in the past, absorb Spain-specific slang, and start handling
roleplay that isn't scripted toward a known outcome.

Weekly themes:

8. Opinions & debate — agree/disagree, hedging, softening disagreement
9. Past-tense storytelling I — anecdotes, `pretérito` vs `imperfecto`
10. Past-tense storytelling II — longer narratives, sequencing events
11. Spain slang integration I — everyday `coloquial` vocabulary
12. Spain slang integration II — expressions, fillers, tone
13. Current events & news — reacting to a headline, forming a take
14. Less-scripted roleplay I — negotiating, complaining, resolving a problem
15. Less-scripted roleplay II — mixed review, open-ended scenarios

**Anki focus:** slang/`coloquial` expressions, past-tense irregulars,
opinion-framing phrases (`a mi modo de ver`, `lo que pasa es que…`).

**Gliglish:** scenarios with a twist — an unexpected complication, a
disagreement to navigate, less predictable phrasing from the AI partner.

---

## Fase 3 — Simulación Madrid

**2026-11-01 → 2026-12-31 · ~8.7 weeks (weeks 16–24)**

**Goal:** simulate actually living there. Bureaucracy vocabulary goes live,
not theoretical. Apartment-hunting and flatmate dynamics. Bar and social
speed — faster, less predictable, more overlapping conversation.

Weekly themes:

16. Bureaucracy I — `NIE`, `empadronamiento`, what the paperwork means
17. Bureaucracy II — `cita previa`, appointments, forms, official phone calls
18. Banking & housing admin — opening an account, contracts, utilities
19. Apartment hunting — viewings, questions to ask, negotiating rent
20. Flatmate scenarios I — house rules, chores, minor conflicts
21. Flatmate scenarios II — social dynamics, hosting, boundaries
22. Bar & social speed I — ordering fast, group conversation, interjecting
23. Bar & social speed II — faster/unpredictable back-and-forth, banter
24. Full simulation — mixed review across all of Phase 3, arrival-week drills

**Anki focus:** bureaucracy nouns and set phrases (`NIE`, `empadronamiento`,
`cita previa`, `contrato de alquiler`, `domiciliación bancaria`), flatmate/
housing vocab.

**Gliglish:** the least scripted tier — bar noise, interruptions, real-speed
back-and-forth, the actual Madrid errands.

---

## If you fall behind

Nothing resets. There is no streak counter anywhere in this app on purpose —
the only thing tracked is whether a given Monday–Sunday week cleared 60
minutes. Miss a day: just do the next one. Miss a whole week: the week after
starts fresh at zero, same as every other week always does. If a day is going
to be zero anyway, the 3-minute minimum-viable-day option (5 cards + one voice
exchange) almost always fits — reach for that before skipping entirely, but
skipping entirely is also fine. The goal is arriving in Madrid in January 2027
still practicing, not a perfect record.
